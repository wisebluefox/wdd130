Arizona

Arizona is a dessert state. The land of cactuses, red and pink sunsets, and flash floods. This image was taken after a freak storm that hit the area. The ground had a fresh wet mineral scent, the plants gained a greener hue under the red setting sun, after a 10 minutes flash flooding storm. Arizona is not for the weak in heart. If you cannot stand extreme heat temperatures 75% of the year and then extreme cold temperatures the other 25%, then do not move to Arizona. One of the greatest benefits of Arizona is its location. Being next to the Mexico border makes it easy to transverse across borders and experience authentic Mexican culture and food. A wonderful place ful of snowbirds.
