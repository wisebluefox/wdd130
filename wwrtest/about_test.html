The Viking Rafters

Our Company

The Viking Rafters is the rafting company for you. We are built on the proud history of ancient Viking explorers, trained in Iceland, and certified to guide others through thrilling outdoor adventures. Join us as we continue the legacy of courage, exploration, and teamwork.

History

Our love for adventure began generations ago. Inspired by Viking explorers and strengthened through outdoor leadership training, Viking Rafters grew into a company dedicated to safe and exciting rafting experiences.
